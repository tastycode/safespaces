    Safe-Space Policy

    You are welcome in this space, but will be refused service if you fail to abide by our standards of inclusivity.

    1. All ages are welcome here. Prejudice or making fun of someone's age is inappropriate.
    2. All bodies are welcome here. Regardless of disability or size. Prejudice or humor in regards to this are inappropriate.
    3. All ethnicities, nationalities, and races are welcome here.
    4. All genders and gender-expressions are welcome here.
    5. All sexual identities and orientations are welcome here.

    Anyone making light of or otherwise tampering with the spirit of these standards will be referred to this policy. Any further complaints will result in being asked to leave.
